Privacy Policy

Gigil · last updated 23 July 2026

Gigil does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. There is no account, no server, and no analytics. Everything happens on your own device.

Photos

You may choose a photo to build a target from. That photo is read and processed entirely on your device — the face mapping runs locally in the app. Your photo is never uploaded, never sent anywhere, and never stored by us. When you close the app or load a different photo, it is gone. We never see it.

What we collect

Nothing. Specifically, Gigil does not collect or process:

  • Names, email addresses, or any account information — there are no accounts.
  • Location, contacts, or device identifiers.
  • Usage analytics, crash reports, or advertising identifiers.
  • The photos you choose, or anything derived from them.

Network

The app runs offline once installed. It does not send your data to us or to any third party, because it has nowhere to send it — there is no backend.

Third parties

Gigil contains no advertising, no third-party analytics, and no social or tracking SDKs. The only optional outbound link is a support (“buy me a coffee”) button, which simply opens Ko-fi in your browser if you tap it; tapping it is entirely up to you.

Children

Gigil depicts stylised, cartoon violence and is not intended for children. It is not directed to anyone under 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from anyone — because we do not collect information at all.

Your responsibility

Because photos never leave your device, the choice of what to upload is entirely yours. Please do not use photos of other people without their consent.

Changes
